F1 Haptic Trailer

Feel the race in your hands. Apple's Formula 1 haptic trailer, rebuilt for Android — every vibration synced to the action. Turn your volume up and hit play.
PC Users: This experience is designed for mobile devices with haptic feedback. On desktop, you'll only get audio without vibrations.

Setup for the best experience

  • Turn off Silent / Do Not Disturb mode
  • Turn your volume up
  • Enable vibration / haptics in your device settings
  • Make sure battery saver / power saving mode is off

Tap the button and feel for a buzz.
